Investing Demystified

Investing. Stocks. Shares. Equities. Bonds. ISAs. These are all terms that are banded about when it comes to personal finance and financial planning. But what do they actually mean? What is investing useful for? And how do you "start investing"?

If you're more of a visual person then check out Lars Kroijer's fantastic 'Investing Demystified' series on YouTube. This is simply a summary of what he talks about, with some insights specific to the UK

You can't beat the market

When you're approaching investing as an individual, you need to take a step back and think about what you're up against. There are thousands upon thousands of companies who are experts at this kind of thing, have access to all the latest research, employ hundreds of analysts to look into every detail of a stock and every way they can make money from it. You have a smartphone, access to the internet, and that's about it. If you think you can compete at this level then this blog post isn't for you, this is for people who want to go about investing in a sensible and sustainable way, while managing their risk.
